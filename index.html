Rules:

Fizz-Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become 'fizz'
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become 'pong'
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 become 'fizz-pong'
